You've Made a Fool of Death With You're Beauty

By Akwaeke Emezi

This Book is so flipping good. Cream of the crop romance. You've Made a fool of death with you're beauty(long title i know) tells the story of a young widower from brooklyn, Feyi Adekola. Basicaly her best friend Joy helps push her to get back into the dating world and one things leads to another till she finds herself spending the week abroad with a new "friend, in his family home, and then finds herself to falling in love with his very lovely father. The author Akwaeke Emezi is who I really love tho. Imma always look for my people and they are.Emezi is a young Nigerian creative that I just know is gonna do big things"

Les Characters

Joy: She's kinda annoying and certainly makes bad choices but you gotta love her boldness

Alim: literally the most perfect being ever. He's a queer, celebrity chef whose also a widower

Beware!!! The ending had me really mad also, i recommend reading it alone. It's a bit wattpad like but that's just how i like it
